Slime Escape

2D Action Platformer

Overview

Slime Escape is a 2D pixel art platformer and my very first game developed in Unity. In this single-player game, you control a plucky little slime blob that breaks free from its cage in a high-security laboratory. To escape, the slime must navigate a series of challenging rooms filled with deadly hazards like spikes and spinning blades. A key feature of the game is the slime’s unique ability to squeeze through narrow vents, allowing it to bypass obstacles and explore hidden areas. Slime Escape was not only a fun creative project but also an important learning experience in game development, combining level design, character mechanics, and custom artwork.
